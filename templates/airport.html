About New York City Airports

New York City is a popular tourist destination for travelers around the world, as well as a global business hub. City visitors, especially those traveling outside of the country, are most likely to opt for a flight into one of the major airports in the NY/NJ region.

There are three major airports that serve the New York Metropolitan Area, while there are smaller, nearby airports open to the public.

Covering 4,930 aces of land, JFK is the busiest aiport in the New York area. It has been long recognized as the premier U.S. gateway for passengers.
