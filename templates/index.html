To New York, with LOVE

Travel ChiViMe

Mision Statement

To bring NYC travelers within global reach.

Vision

It is difficult to find resources that show airport analysis and fare pricing that are accessible to to the public. In cases where the data is available, it doesn't seem to be in a dashboard or analyzed format.

Considering:

  • The NYC Open Data Policy
  • Lack of open source, analyzed data on airports, flights, and fares
This project looks to analyze information about NYC airports, flights and fares

We are REALLY passionate about airports (they are a door to Narnia that you can only get into by going through TSA) - a real world of wonder
