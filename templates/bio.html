Discover ChiViMe

We know NYC flights.

Mission Statement

To bring global travelers within NYC reach.

Vision

ChiViMe aims to a help drive quality travel decesions for NYC tourists seeking a flight to the wonderful New York City, NY. The objective is acheived by analyzing information about NYC airports, flights and fares.

Sources

  • Airport CSV files from Airport IQ 5010
  • Amadeus Travel Innovation Sandbox (Low-Flight Fares Search).
  • Port Authority data on JFK, LGA, EWR, and New York Stewart International Airport (SWF)
